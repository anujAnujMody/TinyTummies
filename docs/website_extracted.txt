🌱 1. THE SOUL OF TINY TUMMIES (READ THIS FIRST)
Tiny Tummies is not a food business.
It is built around a daily emotional moment in every household.
A parent standing in the kitchen every morning…wondering if what they are packing is good enough.
Not just filling the box.But nourishing their child.
There is love in that moment.But also stress. Uncertainty. Repetition. Guilt.
And on the other side of that lunchbox…
A child who:
Gets bored easily
Rejects food quickly
Is drawn to taste, color, and fun
💡 The Core Realization
Children don’t reject healthy food.
They reject:
Monotony
Poor presentation
Lack of excitement
And parents don’t fail at nutrition.
They struggle with:
Time
Consistency
Daily decision fatigue
🎯 Why Tiny Tummies Exists
Tiny Tummies was created to bridge this gap.
To take away the daily stress from parents…and replace it with confidence, trust, and joy.
To turn food from:👉 A struggleinto👉 A habit kids actually look forward to
🎨 2. DESIGN PHILOSOPHY (CRITICAL FOR YOUR DESIGNER)
Before a single pixel is placed, the designer must understand:
🧠 What should the website FEEL like?
When a parent opens your website, they should feel:
“This looks safe”
“This looks clean”
“This is made for kids like mine”
“I can trust these people with my child’s food”
🎭 Emotional Tone Balance
Element
Tone
Kids
Playful, joyful
Parents
Calm, reassuring
Brand
Clean, trustworthy
🎨 Visual Language
Soft rounded edges → safety & warmth
Clean spacing → hygiene & clarity
Bright accents → energy & kids appeal
Real food imagery → trust
🚫 What to avoid
Loud cluttered designs
Cartoon overload (kills trust)
Corporate stiffness
Stock photos that feel fake

Content Experience:
